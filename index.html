Anubhav Mittal

Senior Undergrad in Electrical Engineering

Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur

I am a senior undergraduate at IIT Kanpur majoring in Electrical Engineering with a second major in Computer Science. I am interested in solving problems in Machine Learning, Convex Optimization and Computer Vision.

Presently, I am a research assistant at IITK's SPiN Lab, working with Prof. Ketan Rajawat on problems in online convex optimization. We are currently working on my first paper, which we plan to submit soon.

Prior to this, I have worked with Prof. Satyadev Nandakumar, where we focused on developing a principled intuition behind Hamiltonian Monte Carlo and its optimal implementations. I also had the chance to work with Prof. Vinay P. Namboodiri and Ravindra Yadav on video generation using VAEs. In the summer of 2018, I was an intern in the Isolation Product Line of Texas Instruments, India, working with Koteshwar Rao to improve upon the design of TI's digital isolators.
